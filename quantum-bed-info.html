Far Infrared Light

Radiates far infrared thermal energy, a time-tested healing method rooted in ancient traditions. This gentle warmth deeply permeates, activating cellular pathways to stimulate the mitochondria. It fosters enhanced blood flow, detoxification, lymphatic renewal, pain reduction, and serene relaxation.

Far-infrared (FIR) light, with wavelengths of 3-15 µm, penetrates up to 1.5 inches beneath the skin, improving circulation to nourish tissues, reduce inflammation, relax muscles, and accelerate tissue recovery. FIR also boosts cellular metabolism and detox through perspiration, alleviating pain, stress, and fatigue.

Red light, ranging from 620-700 nm, targets shallower skin layers, promoting collagen synthesis, enhancing skin vitality, and speeding wound repair. By increasing mitochondrial ATP production, it supports cellular regeneration and reduces inflammation, amplifying FIR’s deeper benefits.

Blue light, spanning 400-500 nm, works on the skin’s surface, leveraging antibacterial properties to combat acne-causing microbes and improve skin clarity. It also supports mood balance and sleep regulation by influencing circadian rhythms, enhancing the Quantum Wellness Bed’s calming effects.

Negative Ions

Additionally, negative ions bolster cellular health, promoting detoxification and reducing inflammation, which helps ease fatigue and respiratory challenges.
